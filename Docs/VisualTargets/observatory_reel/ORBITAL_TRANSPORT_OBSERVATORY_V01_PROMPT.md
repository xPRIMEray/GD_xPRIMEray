Orbital Transport Observatory — v0.1 Visual Target
Purpose

Concept-art target for xPRIMEray observability reel development.

This is not physics concept art.
This is observability instrumentation visualization.

The image should communicate:

computational emergence
optical transport observability
scheduler traversal visibility
dual-reality comparison
field influence legibility
scientific instrument atmosphere

without looking like generic sci-fi hologram clutter.

Primary Scene

A cinematic transport observatory chamber viewed from slightly behind and above the operator perspective.

The chamber is geometrically simple:

large hermetic calibration room
dark neutral walls
subtle grid references
clean scientific instrument aesthetic
no fantasy architecture

At the center:
a suspended spherical GRIN field visualization:

translucent amber halo
faint contour shells
soft refractive density gradients
visible but restrained
not glowing like magic

Two synchronized transport views exist simultaneously:

LEFT SIDE:
Straight transport reference.

RIGHT SIDE:
Curved GRIN transport.

The difference between the two is visually legible.

Computational Emergence

The image must show that the renderer is actively traversing the scene.

Visible elements:

partially completed tile regions
active traversal tile border
transparent completed regions revealing final film
untouched regions still muted gray
scheduler emergence pattern visible

Traversal should feel:

methodical
intelligent
instrument-like
calm

NOT chaotic.

Ownership Basin Visualization

Wall impact regions reorganize spatially between straight and curved transport.

Use:

cool cyan/blue transport colors
warm amber field colors
seam boundaries where ownership changes
subtle topology contouring

The ownership basin boundaries are one of the primary visual focuses.
